DESIGN

About

Welcome to our website design course! This course is specifically designed to teach you how to create visually appealing and functional websites from scratch. Our focus is on teaching you the design skills required to create a stunning website, with code being used as a tool to practice and implement your new skills.

In this course, you'll learn everything from the fundamentals of design theory and principles, to how to effectively use color, typography, layout, and imagery to create a cohesive and effective website design. We'll also cover best practices for user experience (UX) design and how to create designs that are user-friendly and accessible.

Throughout the course, you'll have ample opportunity to practice your design skills using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You'll work on a variety of projects that will challenge you to apply what you've learned and experiment with new design techniques.

Our instructors are experienced professionals in the field of website design and development, and they are committed to helping you succeed. They'll provide you with personalized feedback on your work and answer any questions you may have along the way.

By the end of this course, you'll have the knowledge and skills needed to create beautiful and functional websites that meet modern design standards. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced web designer looking to hone your skills, this course is perfect for you. So why wait? Enroll now and start creating stunning websites today!

Structure

  • Introduction to design
    The start of the design course starts by covering the fundamental principles and concepts of designing effective and user-friendly websites. You learn will the history and evolution of web design, including basic terminology and technologies used in the field.

    The design course will then discuss the importance of understanding user needs and behavior when designing a website, and how to conduct user research to inform design decisions. You will learn about the different stages of the design process, including information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and testing.
  • Design principles
    In the Design Principles section of the design course, you will learn about the fundamental principles that underpin effective visual design. You will discover how to create visually appealing and engaging websites.

    We will explore the principles of design hierarchy, balance, and proportion, and how these principles can be used to guide the user's attention and create a clear visual hierarchy on your website. You will also learn about the importance of contrast and repetition, and how these elements can be used to create a sense of unity and coherence in your designs.
